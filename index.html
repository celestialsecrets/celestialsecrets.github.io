Artist Story

"Hello, I am Demet, a 23 year old artist from the Philippines. Currently I’m in my 4th year of university taking Bachelor of Fine Arts and Design.
I've been drawing ever since I was a kid. It started by me just trying to copy anime characters on cards like Pokemon, Dragon Ball, Naruto, and etc. As a kid, I never thought about making art as my career. I really didn’t have any idea at all, it was just my hobby. Growing up, I never really took it seriously, I just drew whenever I’m bored and when I feel like it. Until my senior year in high school, when I discovered more about art and its deeper meaning, that's when I told myself that I will take this path seriously and focus on it.
My dream for the future is to become an artist/illustrator. My goal as an artist is to reach a level where my art would inspire many people. Financially, I want to earn enough through my art, so that my parents wouldn't need to work anymore. They always support me in the path I took, so I want to give back to them".

Part of our revenue will be reinvested straight back into the community. Our number one focus has always been figuring out strategies to bring out the best values for our holders. However, this doesn't mean we are going to take a step back and not do anything. Being decentralized doesn't mean there can't be a leader, so we are here to help drive our brand into a positive direction and execute the decisions that are being made by the community.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. To make it simple, an NFT is like a frontend and a backend of a digital product, the art represents the frontend and the utilities/benefits represent the backend.

Can I just screenshot it?

Hahaha, nope. All NFTs are verified through blockchain technology. Sure you'll get to save our awesome art as your screen saver, but there will be no utilities with it.

What is "Mint"?

Mint is the process of our NFT becomes a part of the blockchain. Basically you become the first owner of the token.

When is mint date and price?

- Date: May 2022
- Price: TBA (Price is dynamic, depending on the price of ETH. Should ETH rise, the mint price will drop accordingly)

How do I purchase?

All Celestials will be available to purchase on our website with Metamask extension. Additionally, you can mint via our smart contract using any crypto wallet. More details coming soon in our Discord channel.
